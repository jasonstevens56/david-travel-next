<p>I ate lunch at <a href=https://3gsdeli.com/ target=_blank rel=noreferrer noopener>3Gs Gourmet Deli in Delray Beach</a>. The food was mixed. We did like the turkey salad (served with fresh tasty rye bread) which is not a usual mainstay at delis so kudos to 3Gs.</p>

<figure class=wp-block-image size-large><img src=https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/20250202_133511-872x1024.jpg alt= class=wp-image-6250/></figure>

<p>As we were leaving, I noticed the bakery case and had to choose between the chocolate babka muffin and a Black and White Cookie</p>

<figure class=wp-block-image size-large><img src=https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/20250202_140443-1024x583.jpg alt= class=wp-image-6251/></figure>

<p>I went with the Black and White Cookie. I think I chose wrong. While this Black and White Cookie was super fresh, it was more of a cake than a cookie. It paled by comparison to some of the <a href=https://localgrubber.com/p/cardinali-bakery-in-carle-place-serves-great-black-and-white-cookies target=_blank rel=noreferrer noopener>best black and whites that I have had.</a> Back to 3Gs, a cake taste and consistency did not really work with their cookie. The frosting also tended towards the cake front. If this was a whole cake with the frosting on top, it may have worked. But as a cookie, it just tasted wrong. It was too soft and felt out of place.</p>

<p>My search continues for the best Black and White Cookie. Let me know if you have a place to recommend.</p>

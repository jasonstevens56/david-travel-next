<p>I was at Wheatley Plaza this past weekend dining at <a href="https://www.barfrites.com/">Bar Frites</a>.  As an aside, we have a great meal there but were disappointed that they removed the Salmon Sliders which is one of our favorite appetizers.  I guess there was not a great demand for that one.</p>

<p>As we drove by the old Stop & Shop supermarket (which we all remember as a Pathmark), we were wondering when <a href="https://uncleg.com/">Uncle Giuseppe’s Marketplace</a> was going to open.  For those of you who have forgotten, Stop & Shop closed in 2024.  We have been waiting patiently but we would love to partake of their gourmet offerings.  Especially after visiting <a href="https://david.travel/lake-grove-wegmans-long-island-powerhouse/">Wegman’s in Lake Grove</a>.  If Wegman’s was not so far, it would be my primary market.</p>

<p>After <a href="https://longislandinsider.com/business/new-uncle-giuseppes-grocery-stores-coming-to-long-island/">some research</a>, it still appears that Uncle Giuseppe’s is still planning on opening during the First Quarter 2026 (so like 9 months from now).  It seems that they are focusing their efforts first on their  Bohemia shop and the pop-up market on Fire Island.  I wish they showed us some more love!!</p>

<p>In the interim, we can always go to the one in Melville!</p>

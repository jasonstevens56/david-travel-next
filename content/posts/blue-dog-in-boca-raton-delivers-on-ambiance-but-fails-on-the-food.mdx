<p>For a change in pace, we headed over to the <a href="https://www.bluedogboca.com/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Blue Dog in Town Center in Boca Raton</a>. It had a great casual environment that claims to be like a New York dining experience. I do not know about that. But it was a nicely decorated restaurant. The highlight of our dining experience was this woman who danced throughout the restaurant while carrying fire batons. This was very exciting</p>

<p>For dinner, we started with the Rustic Meatballs, the Margherita Pizza and the Butternut Squash Fries. We then followed it up with the Crab Cake Sliders.</p>

<p>The bad outweighed the good. The Butternut Squash Fries was so deep fried that it could have been anything. The pizza was like a cracker with cheese. Add the fried crab cake to see if we could over cook anything else. The only good think we had was the meatball.</p>

<p>If we were to go again which is a really big question mark now, I would order drinks and enjoy the entertainment. Skip the food which this restaurant does not excel at!!</p>

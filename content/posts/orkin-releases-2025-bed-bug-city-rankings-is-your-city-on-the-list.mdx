<p>Bed Bug Prevention Tips</p>

<p>Orkin also provides helpful tips for travelers to prevent bringing bed bugs home. The most important advice is to be proactive and inspect your surroundings.</p>

<p>Travelers should use the S.L.E.E.P. acronym to remember key steps:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Survey for signs of bed bugs.</li>
</ul>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Lift and look under items like mattress seams and bed sheets.</li>
</ul>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Elevate your luggage on a luggage rack away from the bed and walls.</li>
</ul>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Examine your luggage carefully when repacking.</li>
</ul>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Place all your clothes immediately in the dryer on high heat for at least 30 to 40 minutes when you return home.</li>
</ul>

<p>Try to be vigilant each time you stay in a hotel.  Bed bugs can create a major headache for you if you bring them home from your trip.  Never leave your suitcase on the bed or the floor.  Elevating it can save you a lot of suffering.  If you are really focused about it.  The best place to leave your suitcase is in the bathtub.</p>

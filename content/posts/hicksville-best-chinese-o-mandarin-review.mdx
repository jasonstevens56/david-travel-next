<p>Stepping into <a href="https://www.omandarin.com/hicksville/">O Mandarin in Hicksville</a> is like crossing a threshold into another world. The ambiance is the first thing that tells you that this restaurant is something different.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_183644-1024x473.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6550"/></figure>

<p>Gone is the familiar Long Island landscape, replaced by an elegant ambiance that whispers of traditional Chinese artistry. Intricately carved wooden panels, warm lighting, and thoughtfully placed artwork create a sophisticated yet welcoming space. This isn't your typical neighborhood Chinese takeout; O Mandarin offers an elevated dining experience focused on the nuanced flavors of Beijing and Hunan cuisine. </p>

<p>From the moment we were seated, the attentive service hinted at the culinary adventure to come. Our server was knowledgeable and passionate, guiding us through a menu brimming with both familiar favorites and intriguing regional specialties. We received both a digital and paper menu which allowed us to peruse through our options.  We decided to embark on a tasting journey, eager to sample the diverse offerings.</p>

<p>The Doornail Dumplings (beef dumplings) were a revelation. Each dumpling were delicious</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_185926-1024x477.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6552"/></figure>

<p>Next, up we ordered the Scallion Pancakes.  This dish came out like a scallion puff rather than a pancake.  The best scallion pancakes that we ever had.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_185603-1024x680.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6551"/></figure>

<p>For our main courses, we tried the Pekin Duck, a classic.  The duck dish was executed with finesse. The presentation was stunning, and the flavor was even more captivating. The skin was lacquered to a beautiful mahogany, offering a delightful crackle with each bite, while the meat beneath was tender, smoky.  The duck pancakes were light and tasty.  This could have been the best Pekin Duck that I ever had.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_190743-1024x944.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6555"/></figure>

<p>We complimented the duck dish with a Kung Pao Chicken.  The chicken had the right amount of spice and flavorful sauce.  They served this dish with Asparagus, which is a change from our traditional orders.  It definitely added created a more balanced dish.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_191930-1024x670.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6556"/></figure>

<p>Even the seemingly simple Shrimp <strong>Fried Rice</strong> was a standout. The rice grains were distinct and perfectly cooked, tossed with a vibrant medley of fresh vegetables that retained their individual textures and flavors – a far cry from the often-mushy versions found elsewhere.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/20250407_191932-1024x610.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6557"/></figure>

<p><strong>Overall:</strong></p>

<p>O Mandarin is a true gem in Hicksville, offering a genuine taste of elevated Northern Chinese cuisine. The attention to detail in both the food and the ambiance is evident. While the prices might be a touch higher than your average Chinese restaurant, the quality of the ingredients, the skillful preparation, and the exceptional service more than justify the cost. If you're looking for a memorable and flavorful Chinese dining experience that goes beyond the usual, O Mandarin is a must-visit. Be prepared to have your perception of Chinese food pleasantly redefined.</p>

<p><strong>Verdict:</strong> Highly Recommended!</p>

<p>I found this <a href="https://theacsi.org/news-and-resources/reports/2025/10/07/acsi-convenience-store-study-2025/">convenience store survey</a> which analyzed customer satisfaction at convenience stores through our the USA. </p>

<p>My key takeaways is that KWIK Trip is number 1 followed by Sheetz and Wawa. Buc-ee’s comes in as the 5<sup>th</sup> highest rated convenience store</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/image-1024x866.png" alt="" class="wp-image-6948"/></figure>

<p>While I have not been to all of these convenience stores, I have to question this study.  I have been to Sheetz, Wawa and Buc-ee’s, Love’s, ExxonMobile, RaceTrac, 7Eleven, BP, Speedway, CircleK and Cumberland Farms.  It is challenging to compare these convenience stores to one another. </p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/image-1-1024x576.png" alt="" class="wp-image-6949"/></figure>

<p>Some of the criteria they used was the quality of the mobile app, mobile orders, loyalty program and frequencies of sales in addition to the food quality and freshness. </p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/20221226_072020-1024x461.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6951"/></figure>

<p>My favorite convenience store is Buc-ee’s.  From my multiple visits, I have found that the customer satisfaction to be the highest of any other store.  People are happy with the bathrooms and proceeding to the food and atmosphere.  I do not know many others where people shop so vigorously for the store clothing. </p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/20241214_161846-1024x478.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6952"/></figure>

<p>Buc-ee’s is not a quick stop.  You can spend easily an hour inside buying clothes, hot brisket, freshly made fudge and beef jerky.  In fact, their beef jerky counter is the size of a deli counter at most supermarkets.  I just do not get it that there are 4 stores rated higher than Buc-ee’s. </p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/20221225_153225-1024x461.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6950"/></figure>

<p>In my opinion Sheetz and Wawa should not be ahead of Buc-ee’s.  I really do like Wawa and it definitely is in the top tier but there is no way you can compare it to Buc=-ee’s.  It is like comparing a Target store to a local Bodega.  They should not be in the same categories.</p>

<p>I encourage people to visit these convenience stores and form their own opinions.</p>

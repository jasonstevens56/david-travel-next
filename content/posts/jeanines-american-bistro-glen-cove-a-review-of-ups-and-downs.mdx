<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-6379"/></figure>

<p>We went to <a href="https://www.instagram.com/jeanines_bistro_catering/?hl=en&utm_source=localgrubber.com&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=jeanine-s-american-bistro" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"><em>Jeanine’s American Bistro in Glen Cove</em></a>. First off, its name is a misnomer since it does not really offer just American/continental food. It has mostly Italian food which is often a good thing.</p>

<p>When you enter, you pass by their wood burning pizza oven. Their pizza chef was a really nice guy who could deliver on high quality pizzas. We loved the pizza and that is the reason to come back</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-1-768x1024.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-6380"/></figure>

<p>For dinner, we ordered a Caesar Salad, New England Crispy Baked Clam Cake and a Veal Florentine. These dishes were underwhelming. The Caesar Salad was bland and the Clam Cake was just a big miss. The Veal dish tasted fine.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-3.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-6382"/></figure>

<p>We were sitting and chatting and the owner came over as well as the pizza chef and bought us dessert which were beignets and a Nutella bread strips.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-4.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-6383"/></figure>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-2.jpeg" alt="" class="wp-image-6381"/></figure>

<p>These desserts were just unbelievable. Both of these desserts hit our sweet spots. I have to say the Nutella dessert was the best which was prepared by the pizza chef.</p>

<p>If you headed to this restaurant, definitely eat whatever the pizza chef is cooking. He is the star of Jeanine’s!!</p>

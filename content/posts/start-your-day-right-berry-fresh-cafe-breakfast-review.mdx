<figure class="wp-block-table"><table class="has-fixed-layout"><tbody><tr><td>On the way to see a spring training game in Port St Lucie, we stopped by at <a href="https://www.berryfresh.cafe/">Berry Fresh Café</a> for breakfast.  It was located in a strip mall with décor from the classic breakfast place like Denny’s or some other well know brands.  Not that impressive.<br /><br />The food on the other had was amazing.  It more than made up for the lack of décor. My biggest regret was not going with more people so that we could try additional items on the menu.  Even with restraint, I was so full that I was unable to finish my meal.  It was a great combination of quality and quantity!!<br />We started with their Classic Cinnamon Bun.  I was surprised at first that it did not come with the icing on top but they let you decide how much to place on it.  With that task complete, we thoroughly enjoyed the cinnamon bun.  It was almost straight out the oven fresh and soft.  It took us no time at all to gobble it up.  For the cinnamon bun alone, Berry Fresh would score top marks!</td></tr></tbody></table></figure>

<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/20250316_114649.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6437"/></figure>

<p>We then ordered the Berry Fresh Slam and a small order of pancakes.  The Berry Fresh Slam consists of two eggs, sausage, the pancakes.  I thought that the pancakes would be the small ones since I say some small pancakes being delivered to the next table.  Boy, I was wrong, they were two full sized pancakes.  The Slam was enough for two people.  The eggs also seems to be two healthy eggs.  It came with a side.  I decided to go with the oatmeal rather than the grits.  This was a mistake.  The only mistake we made with the meal.<br /></p>

<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/20250316_115542-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6436"/></figure>

<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/20250316_115544.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6435"/></figure>

<p>The pancakes were as close to perfection as we could find.  They were golden on top and on bottom.  All too often, we have some pancakes partially burnt or undercooked.  Here they were golden.  These pancakes were fluffy and tasty.  You can not ask for anything more.  I had mine with the bananas baked in which I think just completes the dish.  My wife had them with chocolate chips.  She loves when the chips partially melt to add that chocolate flavor to the pancake.  Guess what, the chips were partially melted!!<br /></p>

<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://david.travel/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/20250316_1155350.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-6438"/></figure>

<p>On whole, this was the perfect breakfast.  I could not ask for a better meal.  I have to tip my hat to the folks at Berry Fresh Café!!</p>

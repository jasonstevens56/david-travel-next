<p>I am headed on a trip for 5 day and need to decide if I am going to take an Uber to the airport or drive to the airport.  If I park at the airport, the question is should I park at airport parking (either long term or economy parking) or at a parking lot off the airport grounds?</p>

<p>I am thinking that I have to research this but either way, I need to minimize potential theft to my car.</p>

<p>Other points to consider:</p>

<ul class=wp-block-list>
<li><strong>Compare prices and options:</strong> Check different parking lots, including on-site and off-site options, to find the best value for your needs. </li>

<li><strong>Book in advance:</strong> Make a reservation to secure a spot, especially if traveling during peak times. </li>

<li><strong>Off-site parking:</strong> Often cheaper and less crowded, but may require a shuttle to reach the terminal. </li>

<li><strong>Parking space selection:</strong> Choose a well-lit area near surveillance cameras to deter theft. </li>

<li><strong>Remove valuables:</strong> Take out any visible valuables from your car to minimize risk. </li>

<li><strong>Consider extra services:</strong> Check if valet parking or other convenient options are available. </li>

<li><strong>Security measures:</strong> Ensure your car is properly locked and consider using a steering wheel lock. </li>

<li><strong>Check for coupons:</strong> Look for deals or discounts on airport parking</li>
</ul>
